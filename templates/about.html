About the National Association of Ankpa Students

Our Story

Founded in 2005, the National Association of Ankpa Students (NAAS) has grown to become a leading student organization dedicated to the academic, professional, and personal development of students from Ankpa Local Government Area.

What began as a small group of students with a vision has now flourished into a vibrant community of scholars, professionals, and change-makers committed to making a difference in our society.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for students from Ankpa to excel academically, develop leadership skills, and contribute meaningfully to society while preserving our cultural heritage.

Our Vision

To be the foremost student association that nurtures well-rounded individuals who will become leaders and positive change agents in their respective fields and communities.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our actions and decisions

Academic Excellence

We are committed to fostering a culture of learning and intellectual growth among our members.

Community Service

We believe in giving back to our community and making a positive impact on society.

Inclusivity

We welcome and value all students regardless of their background, beliefs, or status.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and embrace new ideas to drive positive change.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and partnerships to achieve common goals.

Our Journey

Milestones in NAAS's history

2005

NAAS was founded by a group of 15 students from various institutions who shared a common vision for unity and progress.

2008

First official constitution was drafted and adopted, establishing the framework for the association's operations.

2012

Launched the NAAS Scholarship Program, providing financial assistance to outstanding but indigent students.

2016

Organized the first NAAS Leadership Summit, bringing together over 200 students for capacity building.

2020

Pivoted to virtual events during the pandemic, ensuring continuity of programs and support for members.

2023

Celebrated 18 years of impact with over 2,000 members across 25 institutions nationwide.
