Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NAAS

Membership is open to all students of Ankpa origin in tertiary institutions. Simply fill out the registration form on our website, provide the required documents, and pay the membership fee. Once verified, you'll receive your membership details via email.

NAAS members enjoy numerous benefits including access to scholarships, mentorship programs, career development workshops, networking opportunities, social events, and a supportive community. Members also receive regular updates about job opportunities, internships, and educational resources.

Scholarship applications typically open at the beginning of each academic session. You'll need to be a registered member of NAAS with a minimum CGPA of 3.5 (or its equivalent). The application process includes submitting your academic transcripts, a personal statement, and sometimes an interview. Check our website or contact the scholarship committee for specific application deadlines and requirements.

We're always looking for enthusiastic volunteers! You can sign up to volunteer through our website's volunteer portal or by contacting the events committee. No prior experience is necessary for most volunteer positions, and it's a great way to develop new skills while contributing to the community.

We welcome partnerships with organizations and individuals who share our vision. Please send an email to partnerships@ankpastudents.org with details about your organization and the type of partnership or sponsorship you're interested in. Our partnership team will review your proposal and get back to you with available opportunities.
